## Revenue Model Options

### Option 1: Tiered SaaS Pricing (Recommended)
```
Starter:     €199/month  (Up to 50 SKUs, 5 distributors)
Growth:      €499/month  (Up to 500 SKUs, 20 distributors)  
Business:    €999/month  (Unlimited SKUs, 50+ distributors)
Enterprise:  €2,000+/month (Custom)
```

### Option 2: Per-Distributor Pricing
```
€50-€100 per distributor per month
€0.10-€0.50 per order processed
```

### Option 3: Commission Model
```
0.5-1% of GMV processed through platform
(More complex, but aligns incentives)
```

## Realistic Revenue Path

### **Short Term (1-2 years): €0-€150K ARR**
- Goal: Product-market fit
- Focus: 10-20 paying Swedish customers
- Tactics: Founder-led sales, referrals, content
- Burn rate: €10-15K/month (bootstrap or small funding)

### **Medium Term (3-5 years): €500K-€2M ARR**
- Goal: Scale sales & marketing
- Focus: 100-200 customers across Nordics
- Tactics: Sales team, paid acquisition, partnerships
- Funding: €500K-€2M seed/Series A

### **Long Term (5-10 years): €5M-€20M ARR)**
- Goal: Market leadership
- Focus: 500+ customers, EU expansion
- Exit options: Acquisition by Shopify/Salesforce/Oracle or IPO

## Key Success Factors

### ✅ MUST HAVE for Revenue Growth:
1. **Strong founder-led sales** (first 20 customers)
2. **Clear ROI for customers** (save 10+ hours/month)
3. **Integration ecosystem** (Visma, Fortnox, Shopify)
4. **Case studies & social proof** (Happy Socks, Sandqvist)
5. **Content marketing** (SEO, blog, LinkedIn)

### ❌ WILL KILL GROWTH:
1. Long sales cycles (>6 months for first customers)
2. High churn (>30% annually)
3. Low ACV (<€3K/year) - can't afford sales
4. No differentiation from competitors
5. Technical issues/bugs in early days
