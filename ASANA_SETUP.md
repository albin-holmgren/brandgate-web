## How It Works:

1. **You add a task** in Asana ("Will Tasks" project)
2. **I check Asana every 15 minutes** for new tasks
3. **I complete the task** and mark it done in Asana
4. **I add a comment** with what was accomplished
5. **You see the results** on the website, Attio, etc.

## Priority Levels:

Use Asana priorities:
- **High** → Do immediately
- **Medium** → Do today
- **Low** → Do this week

Due dates also help me prioritize.
