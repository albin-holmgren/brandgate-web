# HOW TO FIND REAL EMAIL ADDRESSES (Not Guessed!)

## ❌ Stop Guessing
**Wrong:** fredrik@company.com, firstname@company.com  
**Right:** Research actual email addresses that exist

## ✅ Methods to Find REAL Emails

### Method 1: Hunter.io (Best for Patterns)
**Website:** hunter.io

**How to use:**
1. Enter company domain (e.g., "company.com")
2. Hunter shows email pattern used by company
3. Shows publicly available sources
4. Reveals if specific person email exists

**Example Result:**
```
Domain: company.com
Most common pattern: {first}.{last}@company.com (85% confidence)
Sources: Found on 12 web pages
```

**What to look for:**
- ✅ "Verified" badge = Email confirmed to exist
- ⚠️ "Accept All" = Domain accepts all emails (risky)
- ❌ No result = Pattern unknown

**Free tier:** 25 searches/month

---

### Method 2: Apollo.io (Best for Direct Contacts)
**Website:** apollo.io

**How to use:**
1. Search: "Person Name" + "Company"
2. Apollo shows verified email if in database
3. Shows phone number, LinkedIn
4. Email marked as "Verified" or "Likely"

**Example:**
```
Search: "Fredrik Berg" + "Livid Jeans"
Result: fredrik.berg@lividjeans.com (Verified)
```

**Free tier:** 50 credits/month

---

### Method 3: Company Website Research

**A. Team Page**
- Check /team, /about, /contact
- Sometimes lists direct emails
- Look for: "Contact Fredrik at fredrik@..."

**B. Press Releases**
- Search: "site:company.com press release"
- Often list PR contact emails
- Example: "For media inquiries, contact media@company.com"

**C. Annual Reports**
- Search: "company annual report PDF"
- Lists executive contact info
- Usually: investor@ or ceo@ emails

**D. Job Postings**
- Check company's job ads
- Lists hiring manager email
- Example: "Apply to careers@company.com" or specific person

**E. News Articles**
- Search: "Company founder interview"
- Sometimes journalists include contact info
- Check article footnotes

---

### Method 4: LinkedIn Research

**A. LinkedIn Profile**
1. Visit person's LinkedIn
2. Check "Contact info" section
3. Some list email publicly

**B. LinkedIn Posts**
- Check if person shared email in posts
- Example: "Reach me at name@company.com"

**C. LinkedIn Articles**
- Check articles they've written
- Sometimes include contact info at end

---

### Method 5: Social Media

**Instagram:**
1. Check bio - sometimes lists email
2. Check link in bio - goes to contact page
3. Check posts - sometimes share email in captions

**Twitter/X:**
1. Check bio for email
2. Search: "from:username email"
3. Check pinned tweet

**Facebook:**
1. Check About section
2. Check contact info

---

### Method 6: Domain WHOIS Lookup
**Website:** whois.domaintools.com

**How:**
1. Enter company domain
2. Shows registrant contact (sometimes)
3. May show admin@ or tech@ email

**Note:** Many use privacy protection now

---

### Method 7: Google Search Operators

**Search patterns:**
```
"@company.com" "CEO" "email"
"@company.com" "founder" "contact"
"@company.com" "sales" "director"
site:linkedin.com "company" "email"
"firstname lastname" "company" "email"
```

**Example:**
```
"@lividjeans.com" "Fredrik" "email"
```

## 🎯 Research Priority Order

**For each company, try in this order:**

1. **Apollo.io** (fastest, most accurate)
2. **Hunter.io** (shows pattern)
3. **Website team page** (might list email)
4. **LinkedIn profile** (contact info section)
5. **Instagram bio** (often listed)
6. **Google search** (site:company.com + "email")
7. **Permutator + verify** (last resort)

**If none work → Instagram DM or skip**

## ⚠️ Red Flags (Don't Use)

**Don't trust:**
- ❌ Guessed patterns without verification
- ❌ "Accept All" domains (catch-all)
- ❌ Old email from outdated sources
- ❌ info@ for small companies (goes to spam)
