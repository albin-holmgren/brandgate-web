# EMAIL VERIFICATION FIX

## Problem: Guessing Emails = 80+ Bounces

**What I did wrong:**
- Guessed email patterns like `fredrik@lividjeans.com`
- Assumed `firstname@company.com` format
- Didn't verify before sending
- Result: 80+ bounce notifications

**Why this is bad:**
- ❌ Damages sender reputation
- ❌ Wastes time and effort
- ❌ Emails never reach decision makers
- ❌ Gmail may flag as spam
- ❌ Low deliverability

## Solution: Verify Before Sending

### Tools to Find & Verify Emails

**1. Hunter.io (Best)**
- Find: hunter.io
- Search company domain
- Shows email pattern used
- Verifies if email exists
- Free: 25 searches/month

**2. NeverBounce**
- Verify: neverbounce.com
- Upload email list
- Returns: Valid / Invalid / Accept All / Unknown
- Free trial available
- Cost: ~$0.003 per verification

**3. ZeroBounce**
- Similar to NeverBounce
- Free: 100 credits/month
- Good accuracy

**4. Apollo.io**
- Find: apollo.io
- Database of B2B contacts
- Shows verified emails
- Free tier: 50 credits/month

**5. Lusha**
- LinkedIn extension
- Reveals contact info
- Free: 5 credits/month

### Verification Results Explained

**✅ Valid**
- Email exists and can receive mail
- Safe to send
- Expected bounce rate: <2%

**⚠️ Accept All**
- Domain accepts all emails (catch-all)
- Risky but may work
- Expected bounce rate: 10-20%

**❌ Invalid**
- Email doesn't exist
- **NEVER SEND**
- Will bounce

**❌ Unknown**
- Can't verify
- **DON'T SEND**
- High risk of bounce

## New Workflow (Fixed)

### Step 1: Find Company
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator
- Verify: 10-100 employees
- Verify: <100M SEK revenue

### Step 2: Find Decision Maker
- LinkedIn: CEO/Founder/Sales Director
- Note: Full name + company domain

### Step 3: Find Email (Use Tools!)
**Option A: Hunter.io**
1. Go to hunter.io
2. Enter domain (e.g., lividjeans.com)
3. See if company pattern exists
4. Check if specific email is verified

**Option B: Apollo.io**
1. Search for person + company
2. Get email if available in database

**Option C: Verify Guessed Email**
1. Guess: firstname@company.com
2. Verify with NeverBounce/ZeroBounce
3. Only send if result = "Valid"

### Step 4: Send ONLY If Verified
**Send if:**
- ✅ Email verified as "Valid"
- ✅ Hunter.io shows pattern match
- ✅ Apollo has confirmed email

**DON'T send if:**
- ❌ Email verification = "Invalid"
- ❌ Email verification = "Unknown"
- ❌ Guessed without verification
- ❌ No tool confirms email exists

## Alternative: Social Media Outreach

When verified email can't be found:

**Instagram DM (Best for small brands)**
- Many founders check DMs personally
- Casual but professional
- Good response rate
- Free

**LinkedIn InMail**
- Direct to decision maker
- Guaranteed delivery
- Cost: ~$10 per message

**Twitter/X DM**
- If person is active
- Professional tone

**Trade Shows**
- Formex (Nordic design)
- Stockholm Fashion Week
- Direct in-person contact
- Most effective
