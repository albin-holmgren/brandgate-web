## Recommended Email Volume

### Cold Outreach Limits (to avoid spam filters)

**For brandgate.dev (new domain):**

| Phase | Daily Limit | Weekly Limit | Notes |
|-------|-------------|--------------|-------|
| **Week 1-2** (Warm-up) | 10-15 emails | 50-75 | Start slow, build reputation |
| **Week 3-4** (Ramp-up) | 20-30 emails | 100-150 | Increase gradually |
| **Month 2+** (Full speed) | 30-50 emails | 150-250 | Max sustainable volume |

### Why Limits Matter:
- **Gmail limits:** ~500 emails/day max (but cold emails should be much lower)
- **Spam reputation:** Sending too many cold emails hurts domain reputation
- **Engagement quality:** Fewer, personalized emails = better responses
- **Deliverability:** Gradual ramp-up prevents blacklisting

## Response Handling Playbook

### If They Respond Positively:
1. ✅ Reply within 2 hours
2. 📅 Offer 3 specific meeting times
3. 📝 Update Attio stage to "Interested"
4. 🔔 Set follow-up reminder

### If They Respond Negatively:
1. 🙏 Thank them for response
2. ❓ Ask for feedback (optional)
3. 📝 Mark as "Not Interested" in Attio
4. ⏰ Set reminder to check back in 6 months

### If They Don't Respond (after 5 days):
1. 📧 Send follow-up email
2. 💡 Offer different value proposition
3. 📝 Log in Attio
4. ⏰ Set final follow-up for 10 days
