### Daily Tasks:
**Morning (8:00 AM) - SEO Health Check**
- [ ] Check Google Search Console for errors
- [ ] Monitor keyword rankings (track top 10 keywords)
- [ ] Check for crawl errors
- [ ] Verify sitemap status

- Pages indexed
