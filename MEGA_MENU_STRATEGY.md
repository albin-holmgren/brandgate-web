## Brand Guidelines Verification

### Colors
- Primary: #003822 (Dark green)
- Accent: #E5A400 (Gold)
- Background: #F5F5F5 (Light gray)
- Text: #1A1A1A (Dark)

### Typography
- Headlines: Bold, clean sans-serif
- Body: Readable, professional
- Sizes: Consistent hierarchy

### Buttons
- Primary: Dark green background, white text
- Secondary: White background, dark green border
- Hover states: Subtle darkening
- All buttons must be clickable

### Logo
- Use BrandGate logo consistently
- Proper spacing around logo
- Clear visibility on all backgrounds

---

## Mega Menu Design

### Desktop
- Full-width dropdown
- 3-4 columns per menu
- Clear section headers
- Hover effects
- Smooth animations

### Mobile
- Hamburger menu
- Accordion sections
- Swipe-friendly
- Quick access to key pages
