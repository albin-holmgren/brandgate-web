## 🎉 Board Redesigned!

The OpenClaw Operations board has been completely restructured for better workflow management.

---

## 📋 NEW BOARD STRUCTURE

**URL:** https://app.asana.com/1/1213451691700872/project/1213474894327849/board/1213474897291909

### 🚨 URGENT - Today
**Purpose:** Critical tasks needing immediate attention  
**Examples:**
- Website is down
- Payment system broken
- Security issue
- Meeting in 1 hour

**Action:** Check first thing every morning

---

### 🔥 Hot Leads - Respond Now
**Purpose:** Responses from high-value prospects (85+ score)  
**Examples:**
- 🎉 Viktor @ Happy Socks wants a meeting
- 💰 NetOnNet CEO replied positively
- 📞 Outnorth asked for pricing

**Action:** Respond within 2 hours, top priority!

**When created:**
- When someone with lead score 85+ responds
- Automatically created with response details
- Includes suggested next actions

---

### 📧 To Do - Follow-ups
**Purpose:** Regular follow-ups and tasks for you to handle  
**Examples:**
- Reply to question from warm lead
- Send case study to prospect
- Schedule call with interested lead
- Update CRM with new info

**Action:** Handle during the day, mark complete when done

**When created:**
- Regular responses (not hot leads)
- Manual tasks you assign
- Follow-up reminders

---

### ⏳ Waiting For Response
**Purpose:** Outreach sent, waiting for replies  
**Examples:**
- Email sent to Happy Socks (3 days ago)
- Follow-up sent to NetOnNet (1 day ago)
- Waiting for meeting confirmation

**Action:** Review weekly, move to follow-ups when they respond

**When created:**
- After sending each batch of emails
- After sending follow-ups
- Tracks how long we've been waiting

---

### 📊 Daily Tasks
**Purpose:** Recurring daily operations  
**Examples:**
- ✅ Morning inbox check (9 AM)
- ✅ SEO health check (8 AM)
- ✅ Analytics check (9:30 AM)
- ✅ Outreach emails sent (3 PM)
- ✅ Daily report generated (5 PM)

**Action:** Automatically logged by AI, review to see what was done

**When created:**
- Automatically when I complete daily tasks
- Logged with timestamp and details
- Shows complete history of operations

---

### 📝 Content & SEO
**Purpose:** Blog posts, content calendar, SEO tasks  
**Examples:**
- Write blog post: "B2B Distribution Trends"
- Create Swedish translation
- Submit to Google Search Console
- Optimize old blog post
- Research keywords

**Action:** Work on content tasks, move to completed when published

**When created:**
- Content calendar schedule
- SEO optimization tasks
- Blog publishing reminders

---

### 🎯 Optimization
**Purpose:** A/B tests, improvements, experiments  
**Examples:**
- 🧪 A/B test email subject lines
- 🧪 A/B test email body content
- 📈 Optimize lead scoring model
- ⏰ Test different send times
- 💰 Reduce hosting costs

**Action:** Review monthly, implement high-impact experiments

**When created:**
- Monthly optimization review
- A/B test ideas
- Process improvement suggestions

---

### 📚 History & Completed
**Purpose:** Archive of completed work  
**Examples:**
- [Completed] Sent 5 outreach emails
- [Completed] Published blog post
- [Completed] Hot lead meeting booked
- [Completed] SEO audit finished

**Action:** Archive completed tasks here

**When created:**
- Move tasks here when done
- Keep for historical reference
- Track what was accomplished

---

### 💡 Ideas & Backlog
**Purpose:** Future ideas and low-priority tasks  
**Examples:**
- Explore TikTok marketing
- Consider podcast sponsorship
- Research new lead sources
- Future integration ideas

**Action:** Review quarterly, promote to active if valuable

**When created:**
- Random ideas that come up
- Low-priority suggestions
- Future exploration items

## 🔄 How Tasks Flow

### Daily Workflow Example:

**08:00** - I run SEO check
→ Created in: **📊 Daily Tasks**

**09:00** - I check inbox
→ Created in: **📊 Daily Tasks**

**09:15** - Viktor (92 score) responds positively
→ Created in: **📊 Daily Tasks** (inbox check logged)
→ Created in: **🔥 Hot Leads** (follow-up task for you)

**10:00** - You see Hot Lead task
→ Reply to Viktor
→ Move task to: **📚 History & Completed**

**15:00** - I send 5 more emails
→ Created in: **📊 Daily Tasks**
→ Created in: **⏳ Waiting For Response** (track who we contacted)

**17:00** - I generate daily report
→ Created in: **📊 Daily Tasks**

## 🎯 Your Daily Routine

### Morning (First thing):
1. Open **Operations Board**
2. Check **🚨 URGENT** - anything critical?
3. Check **🔥 Hot Leads** - respond to high-value leads first!
4. Check **📧 To Do** - handle follow-ups
5. Review **📊 Daily Tasks** - see what I completed yesterday

### During Day:
- Handle tasks in **🔥 Hot Leads** and **📧 To Do**
- Move completed tasks to **📚 History**
- Add any urgent items to **🚨 URGENT**

### Weekly:
- Review **⏳ Waiting For Response** - send follow-ups if needed
- Check **🎯 Optimization** - plan experiments
- Archive old completed tasks

### Monthly:
- Review **💡 Ideas & Backlog** 
- Plan content from **📝 Content & SEO**
- Analyze **📚 History** for patterns

## 📱 Quick Reference

### Priority Order:
1. 🚨 **URGENT** - Drop everything, handle now
2. 🔥 **Hot Leads** - Respond within 2 hours
3. 📧 **To Do** - Handle during business hours
4. ⏳ **Waiting** - Monitor, no action needed yet
5. 📊 **Daily** - Review what was done
6. 📝 **Content** - Work on when scheduled
7. 🎯 **Optimization** - Review monthly

### When I Create Tasks:
- **Daily operations** → 📊 Daily Tasks
- **Hot lead responds** → 🔥 Hot Leads + 📊 Daily Tasks
- **Regular response** → 📧 To Do + 📊 Daily Tasks
- **Follow-up due** → 📧 To Do
- **Optimization idea** → 🎯 Optimization
- **Content task** → 📝 Content & SEO
- **Random idea** → 💡 Ideas

### When You Complete:
- Move to **📚 History & Completed**
- Or delete if not needed
