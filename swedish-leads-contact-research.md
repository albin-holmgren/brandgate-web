## Research Strategy:

### Step 1: LinkedIn Search
Search pattern: "[Company Name] Sweden [Job Title]"
Example: "Winefinder Sweden CEO"

### Step 2: Company Website
- Check /about-us or /team pages
- Look for management team
- Check press releases for quotes

### Step 3: Allabolag.se (Swedish Business Registry)
- Find CEO/VD (Verkställande Direktör)
- Board members
- Financial reports

### Step 4: Email Patterns
Common Swedish patterns:
- firstname@company.se
- firstname.lastname@company.se
- f.lastname@company.se
- firstname@company.com

### Step 5: Phone Directories
- Eniro.se
- Hitta.se
- Company websites
