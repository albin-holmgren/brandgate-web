# NEW OUTREACH STRATEGY (Effective Immediately)

## The Problem: Generic Emails Don't Work

**Current issues:**
- ❌ info@ emails bounce at 30-40% rate
- ❌ Wrong department receives email
- ❌ No one takes ownership
- ❌ Low response rate (<1%)
- ❌ Wasted effort

## The Solution: Personalized Outreach to Decision Makers

**New approach:**
- ✅ Target: CEO / Founder / Sales Director directly
- ✅ Email: firstname@company.com (personal email)
- ✅ Research: Find specific person on LinkedIn
- ✅ Response rate: 5-10% (10x better)
- ✅ Quality: Higher conversion to meetings

## Target Profile

### Company Size
**Ideal:** 10-50 employees
- Large enough to have B2B
- Small enough to reach CEO
- Growing = needs systems

**Avoid:**
- <10 employees (no B2B yet)
- >200 employees (gatekeepers)

### Roles to Target (Priority)
1. **CEO / Founder / Owner** (best for <50 employees)
2. **Sales Director / Head of Sales**
3. **E-commerce Manager**
4. **Wholesale Manager**
5. **Operations Manager**

## Research Process (Per Company)

### Step 1: Find Company
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator
- Instagram brand discovery
- Trade show lists
- Competitor analysis

### Step 2: Find Decision Maker
Check LinkedIn for:
- CEO / Founder
- Sales Director / VP Sales
- "Head of" roles
- Founder in bio

**Red flags:**
- Only marketing people
- Junior roles
- No B2B presence

### Step 3: Find Personal Email
**Try in order:**
1. firstname@company.com (80% success)
2. firstname.lastname@company.com (15%)
3. firstinitial.lastname@company.com (5%)

**Tools:**
- Hunter.io (find patterns)
- LinkedIn (name info)
- Company website team page

### Step 4: Verify Email
- Hunter.io verifier
- Mailtester.com
- Send test email

## Daily Routine (New)

### Morning (2 hours): Research 10 Companies
1. Find 10 Swedish companies (10-50 employees)
2. Identify decision maker for each
3. Find likely email pattern
4. Document in spreadsheet

### Afternoon (1 hour): Send 10 Personalized Emails
1. Use personalized template
2. Reference specific company/person
3. Send to firstname@company.com
4. Log in Attio CRM

### Target: 10 personalized emails/day
- Week: 50 emails
- Month: 200 emails
- Quality > Quantity

## Email Template (New)

**Subject:** `Helping [Company] scale B2B sales`

**Body:**
```
Hej [First Name]!

I saw [Company] on [Instagram/LinkedIn] and love what you're building.

I help growing Swedish brands streamline B2B wholesale. Most brands I 
talk to struggle with managing retailer orders via email and Excel.

BrandGate solves this with a B2B portal - brands typically save 10+ 
hours/week on order management.

Worth a 10-minute conversation?

Med vänlig hälsning,
Will
BrandGate
```
