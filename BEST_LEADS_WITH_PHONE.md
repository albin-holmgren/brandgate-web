## 📞 HOW TO FIND PHONE NUMBERS

### Method 1: Company Website
Visit: company.com/contact
- Look for:
  - Phone number on contact page
  - "Call us" section
  - Office number

### Method 2: Allabolag.se (Swedish Company Registry)
**Website:** allabolag.se

**Steps:**
1. Search company name (e.g., "Fine Little Day")
2. View company profile
3. Check for:
   - Phone number
   - Registered address
   - Company officers

**Note:** Some info requires login, basic info is free

### Method 3: Google Maps
**Search:** "Company Name" + location

**Steps:**
1. Find business on Google Maps
2. Check "Phone" section
3. Call and ask for the person

Example:
- "Koppi Coffee Helsingborg" → Get café number
- Call: "Hi, could I speak with Anne about B2B?"

### Method 4: LinkedIn
**Check:** linkedin.com/in/personname

**Steps:**
1. Visit founder's profile
2. Click "Contact info"
3. Some list phone numbers

### Method 5: Instagram
**Check:** @company or @founder

**Look for:**
- Phone in bio
- "Contact: +46..." in bio
- WhatsApp link
