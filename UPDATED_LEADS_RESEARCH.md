## 📧 EMAIL VERIFICATION STRATEGY

### Before Sending Any Email:

1. **Verify Email Exists:**
   - Use Hunter.io (free tier: 25 searches/month)
   - Use Apollo.io
   - Check LinkedIn for employee names

2. **Common Email Patterns to Try:**
   - hello@company.com
   - info@company.com
   - contact@company.com
   - wholesale@company.com
   - b2b@company.com
   - firstname@company.com
   - firstname.lastname@company.com

3. **LinkedIn Research:**
   - Search: "[Company] wholesale manager"
   - Search: "[Company] sales"
   - Search: "[Company] B2B"
   - Look for: Sales Manager, Wholesale Manager, Export Manager

4. **Website Check:**
   - /wholesale page
   - /contact page
   - Footer for contact info
   - Press kit / media contact
