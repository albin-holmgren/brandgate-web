## Method 2: Swedish Business Registry (Allabolag.se)

**How to Use:**
1. Go to https://www.allabolag.se
2. Search company name
3. Find:
   - VD (Verkställande Direktör = CEO)
   - Styrelse (Board members)
   - Contact information
   - Financial data

**Example Search:**
- "Winefinder AB" 
- "Greatness Group AB"
- "Noqo AB"
