## Priority Order for Outreach:

1. **High Priority (Immediate):** Companies 10-100 employees, digital-first, growth stage
2. **Medium Priority (Next quarter):** Larger companies, established but looking for efficiency
3. **Strategic (Long-term):** Enterprise accounts, complex sales cycles

---

## Research Notes:

### Signs a company is ready for BrandGate:
- ✅ Multi-channel sales (B2B + B2C)
- ✅ 5+ distributors already
- ✅ Recent funding or expansion news
- ✅ Hiring sales/operations roles
- ✅ Shopify/WooCommerce mentions
- ✅ Complaints about manual processes (reviews, Glassdoor)

### Red flags (avoid for now):
- ❌ Only retail stores, no distribution
- ❌ Single product line
- ❌ Very small (1-5 employees)
- ❌ Pure service business
- ❌ No e-commerce presence
