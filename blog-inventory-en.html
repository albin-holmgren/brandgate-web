Inventory Management for Distributors: Best Practices 2024

W
Will
Distribution Expert

Effective inventory management separates thriving distributors from struggling ones. Too much stock ties up capital and increases risk. Too little stock leads to lost sales and damaged relationships. Finding the balance is both art and science.

The True Cost of Inventory

Inventory costs go far beyond purchase price:

  • Carrying Costs: 20-30% of inventory value annually (warehousing, insurance, capital)
  • Stockout Costs: Lost sales, rush shipping, damaged relationships
  • Obsolescence: Dead stock that never sells
  • Shrinkage: Theft, damage, expiration

Key Metrics to Track

1. Inventory Turnover: How many times you sell through inventory in a year

2. Days Sales of Inventory (DSI): Average days to sell inventory

3. Stockout Rate: Percentage of orders you can't fulfill

4. Fill Rate: Percentage of order lines fulfilled completely

Demand Forecasting Basics

Accurate forecasting prevents both overstock and stockouts:

  • Analyze 12-24 months of historical sales data
  • Account for seasonality and trends
  • Factor in marketing campaigns and promotions
  • Monitor leading indicators in your industry
  • Use safety stock for high-variability items

ABC Analysis

Not all products deserve equal attention:

  • A Items (20% of SKUs, 80% of revenue): Daily monitoring, tight control
  • B Items (30% of SKUs, 15% of revenue): Weekly review, moderate control
  • C Items (50% of SKUs, 5% of revenue): Monthly review, simple reorder points

Technology Solutions

Modern inventory management requires modern tools:

  • Real-time inventory visibility across all locations
  • Automated reorder point alerts
  • Integration with suppliers for automated purchasing
  • Predictive analytics for demand forecasting
  • Mobile access for warehouse staff

Conclusion

Great inventory management balances service levels with cost efficiency. Start with accurate data, implement the right technology, and continuously refine your approach based on results.
